About Me

I graduated from Georgia Tech with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering and after two years learning the ins and outs of frontend web development, Tech called me back to pursue a Master's Degree in Human-Computer Interaction, honing my skills and focus in the fields of User Experience Design and Research.

With my technical background, my skills are broad ranging from UX research, to design, to prototyping, and overall project management. However, while I love crafting experiences of all sorts, my main passion within the design space is VR experience design. For the past three and a half years, I've been the CEO of the ZenVR startup, but never put down my work as a designer and always seek opportunities to work, teach, and mentor in the space.

I challenge myself to explore new technologies and learn new things at every opportunity presented to me and from every person I meet. I have strong leadership, teamwork, and communication skills from my time becoming an Eagle Scout and continue to apply those skills with dedication in all challenges set before me.
