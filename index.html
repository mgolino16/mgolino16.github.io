About Me

I graduated from Georgia Tech with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering and after two years learning the ins and outs of frontend web development, Tech called me back. I am currently pursuing a Master's Degree in Human-Computer Interaction, honing my skills and focus in the fields of User Experience Design and Research.

I challenge myself to explore new technologies and learn new things at every opportunity presented to me and from every person I meet. I have strong leadership, teamwork, and communication skills from my time becoming an Eagle Scout and continue to apply those skills with dedication in all challenges set before me.
